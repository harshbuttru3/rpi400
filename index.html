Indroducing Raspberry-Pi 400

It features a quad-core ARM processor, generous RAM, and ample connectivity options including USB ports, HDMI output, and GPIO pins. With its built-in Wi-Fi and Bluetooth, the Raspberry Pi 400 is perfect for a variety of projects ranging from coding and programming to media streaming and productivity tasks. Its simplicity, affordability, and versatility make it an ideal choice for beginners, enthusiasts, and educators alike.
